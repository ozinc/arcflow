## Quick Summary

- **Compiled artifacts** (binaries, .dylib, .so, .wasm) are freely usable in
  commercial products.
- **Source code** of the ArcFlow engine is proprietary and not distributed.
- **Contributions** to the engine are made through structured Intents submitted
  to the OZ Build Relay, where a server-side AI agent implements changes.
- **You own your ideas** — OZ owns the implementation code.
