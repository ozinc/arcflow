## Questions

**What does "production" mean?**
Any deployment that serves external users, generates revenue, or runs business-critical workloads. Development, testing, personal projects, and internal tools are free.

**Can I start free and upgrade?**
Yes. Same engine, same data, same queries at every tier. Upgrading adds a license key — no migration, no downtime.

**Do PanoNode owners need a subscription?**
No. The $499 covers the hardware and a perpetual production license. Each PanoNode is a fragment — it operates independently and syncs to oz.com/world when connected. Services are optional.

**What happens if I stop paying for Professional?**
Your deployment continues running. You lose production rights and priority support. Your data is yours — export anytime.

**Is there a free trial of Professional?**
Community IS the trial. It's the same engine with the same features. The only difference is the production deployment right.

**Can I deploy to multiple nodes?**
Each PanoNode includes its own license. Professional is per-node. Multi-node coordination requires Replication or Enterprise.
