## Out-of-scope tech debt surfaced

Two pre-existing bugs in the workflow that were latent until v1.6.27
exercised the new path. Not blocking the release; routing back to AF
for follow-up:

1. **Attestation org-plan limitation** — already fixed by `bbb5fa64`
   (`continue-on-error: true`). Future tag-cuts unblocked.
   Mitigation: SHA256SUMS is the canonical verify path until ozinc
   upgrades plan OR arcflow-core goes public.
2. **Manifest renders 90 bytes in CI vs 3333 locally** —
   `render-release-matrix-json.py` likely fails to resolve the version
   from `git describe --tags --abbrev=0` in CI because
   `actions/checkout@v4` doesn't fetch tags by default. Suggest adding
   `fetch-depth: 0` (or `fetch-tags: true`) to the checkout step in
   `publish-release` job. Workaround for v1.6.27: DOC used the
   locally-rendered manifest. Filing as a TODO for the next release-cut
   — not blocking.
